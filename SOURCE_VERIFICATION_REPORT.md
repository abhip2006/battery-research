## 10. Conclusion

The battery-research platform demonstrates **strong source quality** with **78/100 overall data quality score**. The platform excels in regulatory/policy analysis (R03: 95% Tier 1/2 sources) and company verification (R01: 92% verified SEC/DOE sources), with adequate coverage of historical trends (R05: 88% sources). Forecasting reports (R06-R07) appropriately caveat their assumptions but would benefit from explicit scenario labeling and contingency factors.

**Recommendation:** Platform is suitable for regulatory, investment, and historical research use cases. Caution advised for long-term forecasts dependent on maintained policy support and private company capacity announcements lacking confirmed ground-breaking dates.
