IRA Section 45X: Production Tax Credits

Advanced Manufacturing Production Credit for battery components (enacted August 2022)

Credit Amounts by Component

Component Type Credit Amount Phase-Out Schedule
Battery Cells $35 per kWh 2030: 75% → 2033: $0

✓ Sector-Wide Impact

  • $48.3 billion invested in battery manufacturing (as of June 2025)
  • 62,700 direct jobs created
  • Credits are transferable under IRS § 6418 rules
  • No foreign content restrictions (unlike 30D Clean Vehicle Credit)

Technical Eligibility Requirements

Battery Cell Definition

  • Electrochemical cell with ≥1 positive and negative electrode
  • Energy density ≥100 Wh/L
  • Storage capacity ≥12 Wh
  • Capacity-to-power ratio ≤100:1

Battery Module Definition

  • Two or more battery cells in series/parallel
  • Aggregate capacity ≥7 kWh
  • ≥1 kWh for hydrogen fuel cell vehicles
  • Capacity-to-power ratio ≤100:1

IRA Section 48C: Advanced Energy Project Credit

Program Overview

Investment tax credit for qualifying advanced energy manufacturing projects

Total Allocations

  • $10 billion total program authority
  • $4 billion for energy communities
  • $6 billion general allocation
  • Battery manufacturing: $4.8B allocated (48%)

Credit Structure

  • Base credit: 6% of qualified investment
  • Bonus credit: 30% (with prevailing wage/apprenticeship)
  • Energy community bonus: +10%
  • Maximum total: 40% of qualified costs
