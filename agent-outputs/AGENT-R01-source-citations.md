## Data Validation Methodology

### Cross-Reference Requirements
- **Capacity Figures:** Verified against 2+ independent sources
- **Funding Amounts:** Cross-checked between company announcements and investor press releases
- **Facility Locations:** Confirmed via company websites and government announcements
- **Partnership Claims:** Verified through both parties' official communications
- **Operational Status:** Confirmed through recent news (within 6 months)

### Uncertainty Flagging
- **"Not Disclosed":** Information explicitly not shared publicly
- **"Approximately":** Estimates based on multiple conflicting sources
- **"Announced":** Planned but not yet confirmed operational
- **"Under Construction":** Construction started but not yet producing
- **"Defunct":** Company ceased operations with official announcement

### Date Stamping
- All data points include approximate collection date
- Market conditions (lithium prices, etc.) timestamped
- Funding rounds dated to month/year
- Facility operational dates verified
