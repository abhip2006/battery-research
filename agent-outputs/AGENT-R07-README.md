## Methodology Highlights

### Data Sources (All Tier 1 or Tier 2)
- **EV Demand:** IEA Global EV Outlook 2024, BloombergNEF, S&P Global Mobility
- **Grid Storage:** Wood Mackenzie, SEIA (Solar Energy Industries Association)
- **Supply Capacity:** DOE Vehicle Technologies Office, Benchmark Mineral Intelligence
- **Battery Costs:** BloombergNEF Battery Price Survey, NREL Annual Technology Baseline

### Key Assumptions
- **Realization Rate:** 75% (historical industry average: 70-80%)
- **Ramp Curve:** Year 1: 20%, Year 2: 50%, Year 3: 80%, Year 4+: 90%
- **Learning Rate:** 18% (cost drops 18% per doubling of cumulative production)
- **Policy:** Base case assumes IRA maintained through 2030

### Scenario Framework
- **Scenario A (IRA Maintained):** 60% probability
- **Scenario B (IRA Modified -50%):** 30% probability
- **Scenario C (IRA Repealed):** 10% probability
