Strategic Recommendations

For Industry

  • Additional 300-400 GWh capacity announcements needed by 2027-2028 to meet 2035 demand
  • Focus on LFP (cost leadership) and LMFP (energy density improvement)
  • Accelerate project timelines to capture 45X credits before 2030 phase-out
  • Invest in cathode and anode material production to reduce supply chain gaps

For Policymakers

  • Maintain IRA incentives through 2030 to ensure self-sufficiency trajectory
  • Address critical materials gap (electrolyte, separator production)
  • Support workforce development programs for 195,000 projected jobs
  • Avoid sudden policy changes that would jeopardize $198B in committed investments

For Investors

  • 2027-2032 represents optimal window for new gigafactory investments
  • Emerging capacity gap as demand accelerates post-2028
  • Focus on LFP projects (40% market share by 2030, superior cycle life)
  • Monitor policy risk: 35-40% probability of IRA modification

For OEMs

  • Secure long-term offtake agreements to guarantee supply in tight 2028-2030 market
  • Diversify battery sourcing across LFP, NMC, and emerging chemistries
  • Consider vertical integration (cell manufacturing) to capture 45X credits
  • Plan for 75 kWh average pack size by 2030 (up from 70 kWh in 2024)
