Commercialization Timeline

2024

Current State: LFP reaches 40% market share globally. Battery costs cross below $100/kWh threshold ($97/kWh for BEV packs). Sodium-ion in limited commercial production (CATL Gen 1).

2025-2026

Near-Term: LMFP enters volume production (10% market share). Dry electrode coating scaled by Tesla (4680 cells). QuantumScape first revenue from B-sample deliveries.

2027-2028

Mid-Term: Semi-solid-state pilot production begins. Solid-state limited commercialization (QuantumScape, Solid Power). Sodium-ion Gen 2 (200 Wh/kg) mass production. Cost reaches $60-70/kWh.

2029-2030

Technology Bifurcation: LFP/LMFP dominates cost-sensitive applications (53% share). Solid-state scales for premium vehicles (15% share optimistic). Li-ion cost floor reached ($58-69/kWh).

2030+

Long-Term Outlook: Solid-state cost parity potential by 2032-2035 (requires massive scale). Flow batteries mainstream for long-duration grid storage (30-year lifespan). Li-ion continues incremental improvements.
